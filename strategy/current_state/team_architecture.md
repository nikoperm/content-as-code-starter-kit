# Team Architecture

## Organizational Principle

Teams are organized around **value streams**, not functions. Each strategic area has an autonomous cross-functional team responsible for its Business Opportunities.

## Strategic Areas

### 1. Core Connectivity
- **Purpose**: Protect and optimize the base connectivity business
- **Team Lead**: [Name]
- **Focus**: Network quality, pricing optimization, churn reduction

### 2. Secure by Design
- **Purpose**: Build and scale security services
- **Team Lead**: [Name]
- **Focus**: SafeZone, Enterprise Security, SOC services

### 3. Customer Solutions
- **Purpose**: Develop tailored solutions for key verticals
- **Team Lead**: [Name]
- **Focus**: Industry-specific packages, managed services

### 4. Efficient Admin
- **Purpose**: Simplify and automate customer administration
- **Team Lead**: [Name]
- **Focus**: Self-service portals, automated provisioning

### 5. Intelligent Communication
- **Purpose**: Next-generation unified communications
- **Team Lead**: [Name]
- **Focus**: UCaaS, messaging platforms, collaboration tools

## Team Composition

Each strategic area team includes:

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| Team Lead | Strategic direction, stakeholder management |
| Product Owner | BO prioritization, customer requirements |
| Technical Lead | Architecture, delivery |
| Data Analyst | Metrics, leading indicators |
| AI Agent Operator | Content-as-Code management, automation |
