# Executive Summary

## Vision

Transform from a volume-based connectivity provider to a service-led value creator by 2030. The market is shifting — traditional subscription growth is flattening, while demand for integrated security, managed infrastructure, and intelligent communication services is accelerating.

## Strategic Priorities

### 1. Protect the Core
Maintain market leadership in connectivity while improving margins through operational efficiency and AI-driven automation.

### 2. Grow Through Services
Build new revenue streams in four strategic service areas:
- **Secure by Design** — Security services embedded in the network
- **Customer Solutions** — Tailored B2B solutions for key verticals
- **Efficient Admin** — Self-service and automation for SMB customers
- **Intelligent Communication** — Next-generation unified communications

### 3. Transform the Organization
Shift from functional silos to cross-functional agentic teams. Deploy AI as a core capability — not just a tool, but an operating model.
