# Security Strategy — Secure by Design

## Vision

Make security an embedded, revenue-generating service layer — not just a cost center. Every customer should be "secure by default" through network-level protection, with premium tiers available for advanced needs.

## Business Opportunities

### BO 1: SafeZone SMB
- **Customer Outcome**: Small businesses protected from cyber threats without needing in-house security expertise
- **Value Proposition**: Network-level threat protection included in connectivity packages
- **Financial Target**: XX MNOK recurring revenue by 2029
- **Milestones**:
  - Q3 2026: Beta launch with 100 pilot customers
  - Q1 2027: General availability
  - Q4 2027: 10,000 active customers
- **Leading Indicators**: Pilot conversion rate, threat detection volume, customer retention

### BO 2: Enterprise Security
- **Customer Outcome**: Enterprise customers get managed SOC services integrated with their connectivity
- **Value Proposition**: Reduce enterprise security costs by consolidating with connectivity provider
- **Financial Target**: XX MNOK recurring revenue by 2029
- **Milestones**:
  - Q4 2026: SOC platform operational
  - Q2 2027: First enterprise customers onboarded
  - Q4 2028: 50 enterprise customers
- **Leading Indicators**: Pipeline value, enterprise NPS, incident response times

### BO 3: SOC Light
- **Customer Outcome**: Mid-market companies get affordable security monitoring
- **Value Proposition**: Fill the gap between consumer protection and enterprise SOC
- **Financial Target**: XX MNOK by 2029
- **Milestones**:
  - Q2 2027: Product definition
  - Q4 2027: MVP launch
- **Leading Indicators**: Market demand validation, pricing acceptance

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Network-level visibility** — see threats before they reach the customer
2. **Scale economics** — amortize SOC costs across thousands of customers
3. **Trust** — established brand in connectivity extends to security

## Dependencies

- Requires investment in SOC platform (CapEx: XX MNOK)
- Needs partnership with security technology vendors
- Customer Solutions team provides vertical customization
