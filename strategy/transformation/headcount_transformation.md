# Headcount Transformation

## Overview

The transformation requires a deliberate shift of capacity from legacy operations to growth initiatives — without reducing overall headcount. The goal is to **redeploy, not reduce**.

## Three-Track Model (Demo Data)

### Track 1: Legacy Optimization
- Current: XX FTEs
- Target: XX FTEs (reduction through automation)
- Method: AI-assisted operations, self-service migration, process simplification

### Track 2: Growth Redeployment
- Current: XX FTEs
- Target: XX FTEs (redeployed from Track 1)
- Method: Reskilling programs, team rotations, new role definitions

### Track 3: New Capabilities
- Current: XX FTEs
- Target: XX FTEs (new hires for specialized roles)
- Method: Targeted recruitment in security, data, and AI operations

## Principles

1. **No involuntary redundancy** — transformation through redeployment
2. **Skills-first** — invest in training before expecting new outputs
3. **Measure outcomes** — track productivity per team, not headcount per function
4. **AI augmentation** — every team gets AI agent support before human growth
