## Theme Color Behavior

Icons automatically adjust fill color based on slide theme:
- **Light background** (`theme: light`): Dark Blue `#070452`
- **Dark/Statement background** (`theme: dark` or `theme: statement`): Telenor Blue `#00C8FF`
