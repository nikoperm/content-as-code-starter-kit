Risk & Mitigation

# Risk Impact What Could Happen Mitigation Strategy
R1 Legacy Stickiness High Automation targets not met — capacity stays locked in operations. Transformation stalls at 2027 peak without reduction. Front-load AI agent deployment (Agentic WoW peaks at 26 FTEs in 2027). Gate every RTB process for automation eligibility quarterly. Hard sunset dates for legacy systems.
R2 Security Adoption Lag High Safe Employee and SOC Light fail to reach mass adoption. Customers perceive Telenor as a connectivity company, not a security partner. Embed security into M4M bundles (bundled SafeZone/Nettvern in core subscriptions). Use 25% hit rate on security leads as leading indicator. December price increase proved low price sensitivity.
R3 5G Commercial Readiness Medium Network slicing capabilities delayed or customer willingness to pay lower than modeled (HalvNi assumptions fail). Validate with Cold Response-style pilots before portfolio launch. Start with 20+ customers for '5G Bare Data' as leading indicator. Build from proven MC segment outward.
R4 Talent Reallocation Friction Medium People in Run/Transform tracks resist or cannot transition to Growth roles. Skills gap between legacy operations and commercial product development. Invest in upskilling during 2027 transformation peak. Use cross-functional team model (not siloed reorg). Track FTE flow quarterly with explicit pipeline from Transform to Growth.
