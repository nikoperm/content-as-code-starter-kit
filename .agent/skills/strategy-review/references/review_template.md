## Usage Notes

1. **Always fill in the full scorecard** — no skipping dimensions without explicit justification
2. **Bold Moves are mandatory** — every review must contain at least 2 visionary suggestions
3. **Impact Delta must be quantified** — "this would improve things" is not acceptable
4. **Chain of Hindsight is mandatory** — log at least 1 learning per review
5. **Adapt depth to document scope** — an executive summary gets lighter treatment than a full transformation plan, but the structure stays the same
